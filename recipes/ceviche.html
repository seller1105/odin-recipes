Avocado Shrimp Ceviche

This ceviche is made with shrimp, fresh lime juice, and refreshing cucumber. Make it as mild or spicy as you want by adjusting the chili peppers to your taste. Serve this ceviche with saltine crackers or tortilla chips. This recipe is great for entertaining and will last a few days in the fridge.

  • 1 pound cooked shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 cup fresh lime juice
  • 1 cup chopped cucumber
  • 1/2 cup chopped red onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh tomato
  • 1 serrano chile, seeded and finely chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Place shrimp in a large bowl. Pour lime juice over shrimp. Stir in cucumber, red onion, cilantro, tomato, and serrano chile. Season with salt, black pepper, cumin, and cayenne pepper. Cover and refrigerate for 1 hour.
  3. Stir well and serve.
